Who We Are


We are an educational consultancy firm that offers expert guidance and advice to students and professionals seeking to advance their education and career. Our team consists of experienced consultants who have a wealth of knowledge and expertise in various academic fields and industries.

What We Do


At Tigers, our services ranges from general consultations on education and migration or travel (Japa), School applications and admissions, Student permits and Visa applications and guidance, Educational loan applications and Proof of fund services, full and partial scholarship programs, all forms of formal writing (CV or Curriculum Vitea, Personal Statement, Statement of Purpose, Introduction Letters and other formal and official writings), Business registration details. If you never had a record of education after your high school or secondary school education, we can also be of help no matter how long the time frame.

Why Choose Us


We are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality of service. Our consultants have a deep understanding of the education and career landscape and are dedicated to helping our clients achieve their goals. We offer flexible and customized solutions that cater to the unique needs of each client.
